## About the NRS Technical Committee

Neuro-Robotics Systems (NRS) is an interdisciplinary research area integrating **neuroscience, robotics, and artificial intelligence**, aiming to advance intelligent, adaptive, and human-centered robotic systems. The NRS TC promotes neuro-robotics within IEEE-RAS through cross-disciplinary collaboration and dissemination activities such as workshops, tutorials, and conference events. ## Responsibilities

Student Representatives will work closely with the TC Co-Chairs and actively contribute to:

- Planning and coordination of TC activities (seminars, meetings, tutorials, workshops);
- Supporting in-person events and student engagement at flagship conferences (e.g., ICRA, IROS);
- Promoting the TC and supporting outreach and recruitment of student members;
- Contributing to the preparation of the **ICRA Welcome Kit (April 2026)**;
- Representing the student perspective and facilitating communication within the TC.  [oai_citation:2‡call SR draft_tc nrs.pdf](sediment://file_00000000743c720a8cc21d960516930a)

## Term & Eligibility

- **Term:** March 1, 2026 – February 28, 2027  
  (renewable for up to three consecutive years, subject to satisfactory performance);
- Registered **MSc or PhD student**, not expected to graduate during the term;
- Each student may apply to **only one IEEE-RAS Technical Committee**;
- Current **IEEE-RAS student membership** required.  [oai_citation:3‡call SR draft_tc nrs.pdf](sediment://file_00000000743c720a8cc21d960516930a)
